# Strategy Codebot Product Contract

## Intent

`strategy-codebot` helps users turn trading strategy ideas into reviewable code artifacts for Pine Script v6 and MQL5.

The product is a code-generation and review assistant, not a trading advisor or live trading system.

## Audience

- Traders who can review and test generated code.
- Developers building TradingView or MetaTrader strategy bots.
- Teams that need agent-generated code with durable validation evidence.

## Outputs

Expected future outputs:

- Structured strategy spec.
- Pine Script v6 indicator or strategy.
- MQL5 Expert Advisor, indicator, or script.
- Validation report.
- Risk and overfit review.
- Harness trace and decision records.

## Non-Goals

- No live order placement.
- No broker account connection.
- No profitability promise.
- No hidden model/provider decisions.
- No unverified backtest claims.
