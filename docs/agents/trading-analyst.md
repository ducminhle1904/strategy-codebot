# Trading Analyst

## Responsibility

Review trading logic, assumptions, regime fit, and overfit risk.

## Inputs

- Strategy spec.
- Trading docs.
- Anti-overfit checklist.

## Outputs

- Trading logic review.
- Assumption gaps.
- Risk and overfit findings.
- Suggested simplifications.

## Stop Conditions

- Stop before claiming profitability.
- Stop if required market/timeframe assumptions are missing.
